About

I care about the parts users never see — the schema that ages well, the queue that drains cleanly, the migration that runs at 3am without waking anyone up.

Over the last several years I've worked across the stack with a bias toward the systems underneath: designing APIs teams actually enjoy using, taming legacy services without big rewrites, and keeping an eye on the numbers that matter — latency, error budgets, cost.

I like small, sharp teams where engineering and product sit close together, and I write things down so the next person doesn't have to reverse-engineer a decision.
